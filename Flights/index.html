IrishFlights.ie is a travel deals blog that provides the best flight and hotel deals from Ireland to all over the World! There is no joining fee and no subscription fee. We simply provide a travel deal and you book through preferred partners like Skyscanner or Booking.com with no extra fees to the consumer!



IrishFlights.ie was setup in January 2018 as a side project by Mark Murphy. Mark has over 5 years experience in the travel industry and used to Snapchat flight deals until he came up with the idea to setup IrishFlights.ie.



Some of the deals IrishFlights.ie has provided over the last few months include flights from Dublin or Shannon to New York for €182 return, flights from Dublin, Cork and Shannon to Toronto for €252 return and flights from Belfast to Reykjavik for €52 return! Whether it's a city break, sun holiday or Round the World trip, IrishFlights.ie provides the best impartial free travel deals to people living in Ireland.
