About TravelDumb

Travel is an interactive travel assistant to help you with planning your ideal trip.

Sick of scrolling through various internet pages to get what you want? Let TravelDumb take the mundane part out of planning your trip!
Simply register and enter in your interests and TravelDumb will suggest a trip based on your selection and month of travel, it's really that easy!
TravelDumb will only show you what's relative to your trip and if there is nothing suitable, we will suggest something else close to your criteria!
